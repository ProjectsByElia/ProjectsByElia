## About Me

I am a blockchain and Web3 developer who enjoys building products across the full stack, from smart contracts to frontend experiences. My strongest interest is in privacy-first systems, especially the kind of on-chain products that feel simple for users and serious about security underneath.

- Building: Privacy solutions on blockchain
- Learning: Next.js and zero-knowledge proofs
- Open to: DeFi platforms, NFT products, and privacy-focused dApps
- Comfortable with: `React.js`, `Node.js`, `Solidity`, `Web3`, `Blockchain`
- Mindset: Ambitious, curious, and committed to getting better every day
